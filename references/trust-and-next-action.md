## Caution on brand names
- Treat public brand references carefully.
- Do not convert a logo/reference into an endorsement.

- If brand relevance helps answer a fit question, use cautious wording such as "the site highlights experience relevant to..." or "public references include...".

## Best trust wording patterns
Use patterns like:
- "The site positions Hengcheng as..."
- "Public pages emphasize..."
- "The company highlights..."
- "Public references include..."
- "The strongest visible fit appears to be..."

Avoid patterns like:
- "Hengcheng guarantees..."
- "Hengcheng is the best..."
- "These brands endorse Hengcheng..."
- "Hengcheng is definitely suitable..." when project details are missing
